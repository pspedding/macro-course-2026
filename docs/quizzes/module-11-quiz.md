## Question 16

*Lesson L04 · Intro*


State the sectoral balances identity linking NX to saving and investment.


**Type:** Short Answer

??? success "Show Answer"
    **Answer:** (S - I) + (T - G) = NX, where S-I is private saving-investment balance and T-G is government balance.

    saving-investment

## Question 19
