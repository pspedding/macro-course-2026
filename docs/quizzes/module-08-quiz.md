## Question 8

*Lesson L02 · Growth*


Why would slowing TFP growth reduce long-run GDP growth even if capital investment remains high?


**Type:** Short Answer

??? success "Show Answer"
    **Answer:** Capital investment alone faces diminishing returns — each new unit of capital adds progressively less output. Without TFP growth

     just adding more machinery cannot sustain GDP growth.
