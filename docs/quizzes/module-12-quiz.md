## Question 2

*Lesson L01 · Monetary Policy*


A bank has $500m assets and $450m deposits. Assets fall 12%. Calculate new equity.


**Type:** Calculation

??? success "Show Answer"
    **Answer:** -$10m

    New equity = (500×0.88) - 450 = 440 - 450 = -$10m (insolvent)

## Question 3

*Lesson L01 · Monetary Policy*


Why are bank runs self-fulfilling even if a bank is fundamentally solvent?


**Type:** Short Answer

??? success "Show Answer"
    **Answer:** Fractional reserve banking means banks cannot repay all depositors simultaneously — fear of a run causes actual insolvency.

    All depositors withdrawing at once forces distressed asset sales.
