## Question 27

*Lesson L06 · Monetary Policy*


If M grows 10%, V is stable, and Y grows 3%, inflation is:


**Type:** Calculation

??? success "Show Answer"
    **Answer:** 7

    %ΔM + %ΔV = %ΔP + %ΔY → 10+0=%ΔP+3 → %ΔP=7
