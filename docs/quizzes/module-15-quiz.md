## Question 1

*Lesson L01 · Monetary Policy*


How does monetary expansion affect IS-LM equilibrium?


**Type:** Multiple Choice

- **A)** Y↑, r↑
- **B)** Y↑, r↓
- **C)** Y↓, r↑
- **D)** Y↓, r↓

??? success "Show Answer"
    **Answer: B) Y↑, r↓**

    LM shifts right, lowering r and raising Y
